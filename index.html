AI Readiness Audit Toolkit

A practical internal tool for your technology and communications teams to assess how prepared businesses are for AI adoption.

How it works

  1. Capture client/business details.
  2. Score each AI readiness category from 1 (weak) to 5 (strong).
  3. Capture the extended financial-sector governance and GenAI questions.
  4. Review graphs + recommendations and download your report files.

Tip: later you can connect this app to OpenAI in generateAiSummary() in app.js.
